I am Simon Girma. I was born on July 8 2000 in the town of Debre Birhan , which is located 120 kilo meters away from the capital Addis Ababa . I was also raised in Debre Birhan where I spent an unforgettable childhood. Though I have lost all memory of the first two and three years of my life, I remember everything after that. Like any Ethiopian kid, I grew up playing with other kids in my village. Football and hide and seek were the games we played the most. When I turned four, I started learning kindergarten in a Catholic school. After learning there for two years, I started going to another school called Millennium Academy where I completed the rest of my elementary and high school educations upto grade 12.
